01

Overview

Adlytics Ad Blocker ("Adlytics", "we", "our", or "us") is a local DNS ad blocker for Android. Adlytics uses Android's VPN permission only to create an on-device filtering tunnel that helps block ad, tracker, malware, and selected category domains on the user's device.

Adlytics does not provide a remote VPN service, does not route traffic through our servers, and does not sell internet access or location masking.

03

What Adlytics Does

Adlytics uses Android's VpnService API to inspect DNS requests on the device so it can decide whether to block or allow domain lookups according to the user's settings.

This processing is performed entirely on-device. Adlytics does not operate a remote VPN endpoint for this feature.

04

Data We Access & Process

The following data is accessed and processed locally on your device:

  • DNS domain requests needed to determine whether a domain should be blocked or allowed
  • App settings such as DNS server choice, protection mode, pause state, and filter preferences
  • User-created blocklist and allowlist entries
  • Blocked-domain history stored on the device for the user's own review
  • Purchase status information returned by Google Play Billing for premium unlocks
05

Data We Do Not Collect to Our Servers

Adlytics does not transmit the following to our servers:

  • Browsing history or blocked-domain history
  • Custom rules, allowlist or blocklist entries
  • Messages, passwords, or files
  • Payment details or financial information
Adlytics does not create user accounts and does not require sign-in.
06

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your blocked-domain history, custom rules, or filtering settings with third parties.

The app may communicate with:

  • The DNS resolver selected by the user or configured in the app, for DNS lookups
  • Google Play Billing, when a user purchases or restores a premium unlock

These services operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

07

On-Device Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Blocked-domain history
  • Custom blocklist entries
  • Custom allowlist entries
  • Filter and DNS preferences
  • Local consent and premium-unlock state
Adlytics is configured not to back up this app data through Android cloud backup.
08

Exported Data

If the user chooses the export feature, blocked-domain history may be exported by the user to a CSV file in device storage. This export is entirely user-initiated — we do not trigger or access this file.

09

Data Retention & Deletion

Blocked-domain history and settings remain on the device until the user clears them, overwrites them, exports them, or uninstalls the app.

Uninstalling the app removes app data stored in the app sandbox from the device under normal Android behavior.

10

Security

Adlytics is designed to process filtering data locally on-device and to minimize off-device transfer of user data.

11

Children's Privacy

Adlytics is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children to our servers.

12
