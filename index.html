// How It Works

Powerful filtering. Local by design.

🛡️

On-Device DNS Filtering

Uses Android's VpnService API only to create an on-device DNS filtering loop. Filtering stays local and is not routed to a remote VPN server.

🚫

DNS-Level Blocking

Intercepts DNS queries and blocks ad, tracker, malware, and category domains before they can load.

📋

Custom Rules

Add your own blocklist and allowlist entries. Fine-tune what gets blocked or allowed on your terms.

📊

Block History

Review which domains were blocked, stored entirely on your device. Export to CSV anytime you want.

🔑

No Account

No sign-in, no user accounts, no email collection required to use the app.

☁️

No Cloud Backup

App data is not backed up via Android cloud backup. Filtering history and custom rules stay on your device.

Privacy is the
default.

DNS Ad Blocker was built with a simple principle: filter locally and minimize off-device data transfer. Blocked-domain history and filtering settings stay on your device unless you export them yourself.

  • No blocked-domain history or filtering settings sent to our servers
  • No user accounts or sign-in required
  • DNS requests processed on-device for filtering
  • No remote VPN server
  • You can remove local app data by uninstalling the app
